About Air System Coolers Ltd

Engineering Excellence Since 2000

Our Mission

At Air System Coolers, we don't just install equipment; we engineer comfort, safety, and efficiency. Our mission is to provide Uganda's industries and homes with world-class mechanical and electrical systems that stand the test of time.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Engineering Precision: Every project is backed by rigorous calculation and design, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency.
  • Safety First Culture: As NFPA and CFPS certified specialists, we adhere to the strictest international safety standards to protect your people and assets.
  • Local Expertise, Global Standards: We understand Uganda's unique climate and power landscape, tailoring global best practices to local realities.
  • Lifecycle Support: From initial design to 24/7 emergency maintenance, we are with you at every stage of your system's life.

Our Divisions

  • Mechanical Systems: HVAC, Ventilation, and Air Filtration.
  • Fire Protection: Industrial and domestic fire fighting systems.
  • Electrical: Power distribution, installation, and automation.
